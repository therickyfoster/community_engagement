# **❄️ 5. The Seven Types of Steward Signals**

Steward signals manifest in seven archetypal forms.

---

## **1. The Frostline Signal**

A sudden sense of clarity, stillness, or inner winter.
Triggers introspection.

## **2. The Ember Signal**

A warm feeling toward action, small or large.
Often sparked by seeing someone else help.

## **3. The Sproutline Signal**

Curiosity, lightness, new beginnings.
Common in spring-coded spaces.

## **4. The Rootsignal**

A sense of connection to land, memory, or community.

## **5. The Auroral Drift**

Subtle emotional uplift when seeing aurora colors or flowing animations.

## **6. The Echo Signal**

Hearing a phrase or idea that feels strangely familiar.

## **7. The Convergence Signal**

Very rare — a moment that feels mythically aligned, like “I’m supposed to be here.”

---

These signals appear visually, narratively, emotionally, or symbolically.

# **❄️ 6. How the Engine Produces Steward Signals**

Signals emerge organically from the system’s symbolic logic:

### **A. Seasonal Theme Shifts**

Winter invites stillness; spring invites growth.

### **B. Ritual Family Presence**

When Ember rituals appear, warm signals follow.

### **C. Seed Pathway Clusters**

If pathlines align strongly, Whisperline effects increase.

### **D. Constellation Echoes**

Sky patterns drift to form aurora arcs during key moments.

### **E. Frostmark Activity**

Growth or change in the Frostmark can produce subtle ambient cues.

### **F. Lineage Moments**

When a lineage echo activates, the system may display mild auroral shimmer.

### **G. Community Events**

Restoration acts, planting, helping others — all create symbolic “aurora drift.”

**No personal data is ever used.
No one is ever tracked.
Only symbolic, non-identifiable cues feed these signals.**

This is a zero-harm resonance mechanism.
