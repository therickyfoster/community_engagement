# **❄️ 1. Animated Title Schema (Design Notes)**

The Frostmark is the steward’s living symbol — its evolution must feel organic, icy, warm, mythic, and emotionally aware.

**Animation Behavior**
• Title begins as a bare frost rune
• Thin cracks radiate outward
• Green, gold, blue, or amber lines bloom depending on season
• Veins pulse softly like breath
• Final state: a glowing, evolving glyph pattern behind the title

**Color Palette (Living Glyph Set)**
• Ice Crystal White (#E9F3FF)
• Winter Indigo (#1E2C3B)
• Thaw Green (#9ED49A)
• Ember Gold (#E7BC6A)
• Harvest Amber (#C47A45)

**Ambient Effects**
• ice-soft crackling
• frost drifting sideways
• gentle pulse when glyph glows

The Frostmark should look alive — like nature and story blended.

# **❄️ 4. Glyph Anatomy (Five Layers)**

The Frostmark has five symbolic layers that evolve independently:

---

## **Layer 1 — The Core Ember**

Represents vow-root energy.

Winter → Blue-white core
Spring → Soft green core
Summer → Gold core
Autumn → Amber core

Core changes subtly with seasons.

---

## **Layer 2 — The Frostframe**

Represents emotional resilience.

• sharp edges → vigilance
• soft edges → calm
• branching frost → introspection
• melting frost → hope rising
• re-frosting → survival and rest

Frostframe is the emotional mirror.

---

## **Layer 3 — The Seedlines**

Represents renewal and real-world action.

• sprouting filaments → planting
• strong lines → community help
• winding vines → emotional growth
• upward curls → inspiration

Seedlines respond strongly to spring and summer.

---

## **Layer 4 — The Emberveins**

Represents Winterfire and vow alignment.

• weak flicker → Sparkfelt
• pulsing → Emberflare
• flowing gold → Hearthfire
• aurora threads → Dawnfire

Emberveins glow brighter during vow resonance.

---

## **Layer 5 — The Constellation Halo**

Represents lineage echoes and region constellations.

• faint star dots → early region activity
• connecting arcs → active constellations
• bright arcs → shared resonance
• golden arcs → lineage illumination

Halo is optional and only appears after certain thresholds.

---

# **❄️ 5. Frostmark Evolution Triggers**

The Frostmark evolves through symbolic triggers — not scores or gamification.

Key trigger families:

### **A. Seasonal Change**

Every transition reshapes:

• color palette
• edge style
• branch density

### **B. Ritual Activation**

Ember rites → melting and re-freezing effects
Seed rites → sprouting lines
Memory rites → amber ring growth
Feast rites → warm radiance

### **C. Winterfire Events**

Strong influence, igniting Emberveins.

### **D. Lineage Echoes**

Add constellation or ancestral arcs.

### **E. Emotional Resonance**

Smooths, sharpens, or softens Frostframe geometry.

### **F. Mnemonic Expansions**

Root word influence shifts structure subtly.

Each influence is symbolic and additive.

# **❄️ 7. Dynamic Frostmark Behaviors**

The Frostmark is not static — it behaves like a living being.

### **Idle Glow**

Soft, rhythmic pulse tied to Emotional Resonance archetype.

### **Season Drift**

Slow transition between seasonal color palettes.

### **Ritual Echo**

After a ritual, the Frostmark glows brighter for a short period.

### **Lineage Spark**

A tiny spark travels through the glyph after lineage events.

### **Winterfire Surge**

Veins glow intensely for several seconds.

### **Constellation Weave**

Halo stars shift slightly when region constellations evolve.

These behaviors make the Frostmark feel alive and attentive.

# **❄️ 8. Frostmark Personalization**

The steward can choose:

• shape style (circle, square, diamond, snowflake, flame)
• animation intensity (calm, medium, mythic)
• glow preference (cool, warm, neutral)
• visibility (always-on, ritual-only, winter-only)

But the evolution is organic — not user-controlled.

Personalization is aesthetic, not narrative.
